02The Band

An Unlikely
Harmony

When a soulful country singer and a legendary rock vocalist discover they harmonize like they’ve sung together their whole lives, something special happens.

Nashville, TN

Dixon Hall is a modern country band built on the unlikely but powerful partnership between vocalist and songwriter John Dixon Hall Jr. and legendary rock guitarist Terry Glaze. Their musical backgrounds could hardly be more different — but it’s their vocal blend and natural harmony that truly sets the band apart. Hall’s soulful country tone paired with Glaze’s gritty rock edge creates a powerful dynamic on stage, and what started as a collaboration quickly evolved into a full band with a fast-growing regional following.

Vocals · Songwriter

John Dixon
Hall Jr.

A Maryland native with deep roots in soul and country, Hall built his reputation performing across the Mid-Atlantic — with a voice that blends heartfelt storytelling and powerful delivery.

Maryland, USA
Guitar · Vocals

Decades on the world’s stages — the original vocalist for Pantera and frontman of Lord Tracy. Glaze brings a gritty rock edge that meets Hall’s soul head-on.
